Hello world

I have decided to start blogging again. I used to blog rather regularly about 10 years ago, more or less, when I was actively developing my first startup svejo.net. Then I stopped. Blogging requires consistency, time, dedication and even more. However, you reap the benefits much later. I guess that’s the reason why many people stop blogging (apart from moving to walled gardens like Medium, Twitter, LinkedIn, FB, etc.).

Yet, I’ve been observing a slight renaissance in blogging in the last year or two. That, plus the idea that one develops his thinking, reasoing and ideas by writing, made me want to start blogging again. Perhaps, some ideas wanted to come out.

I have also had the idea of building an RSS reader. It has been boiling at the back of my head for almost a year. I even bought a domain a few months ago. I have the (“software engineer”) mantra that one should use what they are building if they want to make something useful. I think that taking an active part in the blogging community would be the best way to experience the genuine needs of an RSS reader user.

So, let’s see what happens.
