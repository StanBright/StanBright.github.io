Hello world

I have decided to start blogging again. I used to blog relatively regularly about 10 years ago. More or less, when I was actively developing svejo.net. Then I stopped. Blogging requires consistency, time, dedication and even more. However, you reap the benefits much later. I guess that’s the reason why many people stop blogging (apart from moving to walled gardens like Medium, Twitter, LinkedIn, FB, etc.).

Yet, I’ve been observing a slight renaissance in blogging in the last year or two. That and the idea that one improves his thinking by writing, made me wanna start blogging again.

I have also had the idea of building an RSS reader. It has been boiling at the back of my head for almost a year. I even bought a domain a few months ago. I have the mantra that one should use what he is building if he wants to make something useful. I think that taking an active part in the blogging community would be the best way to experience the genuine needs of an RSS reader user.

So, let’s see what happens.
