CloverQuotes.com

Famous quotes by famous authors. Inspiration and Knowledge.

2010 Sep

Visit website


The goal of this project was to create the best database of famous quotes. Furthermore, it had to have modern and slick design in contrast with all popular quotations sites that have old and ugly designs overwhelmed with advertisements. I took the site down in 2014 as it could not gain any traction. The lesson learned here is that these kind of websites are all about SEO and not quality of the website.
